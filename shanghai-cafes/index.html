Cafes I've been to in Shanghai

I've visted quite a number of cafes in Shanghai. Usually I'd go to one to study, work, or hang out with friends. I've spent hours on 大众点评 searching for big ones to study at. So I made this visualization to help me categorize them.

made with vanilla js and d3

how to read

SIZE OF CAFE --> SIZE OF BEAN

LOCATED IN WHICH DISTRICT --> BEAN ROAST

GOOD SEATS FOR WORKING?--> AROMA WAVES

ANYTHING SPECIAL? -> TOP-RIGHT SYMBOLS
